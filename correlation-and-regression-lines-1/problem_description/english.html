Task
For a particular scatter plot, the line of regression of $y$ on $x$ is:

$3x + 4y + 8 = 0$

And the line of regression of $x$ on $y$ is:

$4x + 3y + 7 = 0$

Find the Pearson Product moment coefficient, $r$, correct to a scale of $2$ decimal places.
