Given an array of ``N`` elements, find the contiguous and non-contiguous(not necessarily contiguous) subarray of maximium sum.

**Input**  

First line of the input has an integer ``T``. ``T`` cases follow 
Each test case begins with an integer ``N``. In the next line, ``N`` integers follow. 

**Output**  

2 space separated integers denoting the maximum contiguous and non-contiguous subarray

**Constraints:**  

1 <= T <= 10 

1 <= N <= 100,000 

-10,000 <= Ai <= 10,000 

**Sample cases**  

**Input**  

1 

4 

1 2 3 4
    
**Output**  

10 10
